Kachori Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Moong Dal (soaked)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough with flour, salt, and water. Let it rest.
  2. Grind soaked moong dal coarsely.
  3. Heat oil, add cumin and fennel seeds, then add ground dal and spices. Cook until dry.
  4. Make small balls of dough, fill with dal mixture, and seal.
  5. Deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot.
