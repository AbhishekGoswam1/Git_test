Rabdi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 liter Full Cream Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Nuts (Almonds, Pistachios)
  • Few strands of Saffron

Instructions

  1. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan, reduce to half while stirring occasionally.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron. Cook until thickened.
  3. Garnish with chopped nuts. Serve chilled.
