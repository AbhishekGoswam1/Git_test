Lemon-Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cooked Rice
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and onion. Cook until soft.
  2. Add turmeric, red chili, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and lemon juice. Mix well.
  4. Serve hot with papad or raita.
