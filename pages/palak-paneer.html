Palak-Paneer Recipe

Back to Index

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Spinach
  • 1 cup Paneer
  • 1 Onion, Tomato
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  • Blanch spinach, blend into a paste.
  • Heat oil, add cumin, ginger-garlic paste, and onions. Cook until brown.
  • Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chilli, garam masala, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  • Add spinach paste, cook for 5 minutes.
  • Add paneer cubes, cook for 2 minutes. Serve hot.
