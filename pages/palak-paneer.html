Palak-Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Spinach (Palak)
  • 1 cup Paneer
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach, blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onion, and tomato. Cook until soft.
  3. Add turmeric, red chili, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add spinach paste, paneer, and water. Cook until flavors combine.
  5. Serve hot with rice or roti.
