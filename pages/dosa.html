Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  • Prepare the dough with flour, salt, and water. Let it rest.
  • Grind soaked moong dal coarsely.
  • Heat oil, add cumin and fennel seeds, then add ground dal and spices. Cook until dry.
  • Make small balls of dough, fill with dal mixture, and seal.
  • Deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot.
