Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Pasta
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Flakes
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil, add onion and tomato. Cook until soft.
  3. Add oregano, red chili flakes, black pepper, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add milk and pasta. Cook until milk thickens.
  5. Serve hot with garlic bread.
