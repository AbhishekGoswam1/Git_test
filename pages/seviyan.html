Seviyan Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup seviyan (Vermicelli)
  • 1/4 cup Ghee
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Nuts (Almonds, Cashews, Raisins)
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add seviyan, and roast until golden brown.
  2. Add sugar and water, cook until seviyan is soft.
  3. Add nuts and cardamom powder. Serve hot.
