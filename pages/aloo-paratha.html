Aloo-Paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
  • 2 Potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough with flour, salt, and water. Let it rest.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with cumin, red chilli, coriander, garam masala, and salt.
  3. Make small balls of dough, fill with potato mixture, and seal.
  4. Roll out the stuffed dough balls into flat circles.
  5. Heat a griddle, cook the paratha with oil until golden brown. Serve hot.
