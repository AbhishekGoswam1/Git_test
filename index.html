Kadhai Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (sliced)
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper (sliced)
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a kadhai.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add sliced bell peppers and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.
  8. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
