Kadhi-Chawal Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Gram Flour (Besan)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 2-3 Dry Red Chilies
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups Cooked Rice

Instructions

  1. Whisk yogurt, gram flour, turmeric, chili powder, and salt with water.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin, mustard seeds, and dry red chilies.
  3. Add yogurt mixture and cook on low heat until thickened.
  4. Serve hot kadhi with cooked rice.
